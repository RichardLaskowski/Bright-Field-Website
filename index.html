About Us

A Christian church in DeKalb, Illinois


Bright Field Church is a Christian church in DeKalb that will begin Sunday services in August of 2018. We will be one of the many churches in the DeKalb area sharing the truth and love of Jesus with all people. We're made up of families, young professionals, and college students.
